Advanced Counseling Solutions took root in 2003, then fully formed and developed as a private organization in 2010. Advanced Counseling Solutions is comprised of knowledgeable and experienced mental health clinicians who provide health education and treatment to children, adolescents and adults in a compassionate and confidential environment. Our offices serve Mahoning and Trumbull counties in Ohio and surrounding areas.
